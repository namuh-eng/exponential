# Enterprise and self-hosted packaging

Exponential offers two custom enterprise paths:

- **Enterprise Cloud**: hosted enterprise controls, SAML/SCIM, audit exports, security review support, and dedicated commercial support.
- **Enterprise Self-hosted**: deployment in the customer environment with commercial self-host license terms, deployment guidance, upgrade planning, and priority support.

## Community Self-hosted vs Enterprise Self-hosted

Community Self-hosted is source-available and self-supported under the repository license. Teams may deploy and modify it within the license terms, but support, upgrade assistance, custom security review workflows, and commercial indemnity are not included.

Enterprise Self-hosted is the commercial self-host package. It adds negotiated license terms, implementation guidance, support SLAs, and enterprise security review help while keeping infrastructure under the customer's control.

## Contact flow

Public CTAs route to the application signup/contact intent flow (`/signup?intent=enterprise-cloud` or `/signup?intent=enterprise-self-hosted`). Do not embed private email addresses, phone numbers, API keys, or Stripe checkout links in public enterprise CTAs.
